Recognizing Warning Signs

Even with demo gaming, it's important to recognize signs of problematic gaming behavior:

  • Spending excessive amounts of time gaming
  • Neglecting work, family, or social responsibilities
  • Feeling anxious or irritable when not gaming
  • Using gaming to escape from problems or negative emotions
  • Lying about time spent gaming
  • Experiencing sleep problems due to gaming
  • Losing interest in other hobbies or activities

Important Notice

If you recognize these warning signs in yourself or someone you know, it may be time to seek help or take a break from gaming activities.

Healthy Gaming Tools & Tips

Use these practical tools to maintain healthy gaming habits:

  • Set Time Limits: Use device timers or apps to limit gaming sessions
  • Take Regular Breaks: Follow the 20-20-20 rule (every 20 minutes, look at something 20 feet away for 20 seconds)
  • Schedule Gaming: Plan gaming time like any other activity
  • Stay Hydrated: Keep water nearby and drink regularly
  • Maintain Posture: Use ergonomic seating and take stretch breaks
  • Social Gaming: Play with friends or family when possible
  • Diversify Activities: Balance gaming with other hobbies and interests
